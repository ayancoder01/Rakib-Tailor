Common Queries

Questions
& Answers

01

How long does a bespoke suit take?

Normally between 4 to 6 weeks, depending on the complexity and fabric choice. Our master tailors dedicate over 45 hours of handwork to each commission.
02

Do you offer home consultations?

Yes, we provide exclusive home and office consultations for our premium clientele. Travel within a 30-mile radius is complimentary.
03

What fabrics do you work with?

Exclusively world-class mills: Loro Piana, Vitale Barberis Canonico, Holland & Sherry. Every fabric is selected for its provenance, weight, and tactile quality.
04

What is your fitting process?

We schedule a minimum of two fittings — a basted fitting and a forward fitting — before the final delivery to ensure an anatomically perfect garment.
